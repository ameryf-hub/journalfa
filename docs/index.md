# Welcome to APEX BREAKOUT

## Overview
APEX BREAKOUT is an **Institutional Volume Surge Screener** that scans ~1,600 stocks across S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, and Russell 1000 indices.

### Key Features
- 📊 **Volume Surge Detection** — Real-time institutional activity signals
- 🎯 **5-Criteria Overlay** — Revenue growth, EPS growth, 52W base, volume ratio, breakout score
- 🚀 **Breakout Score** — Composite 0-100 ranking system
- 📈 **Live Data** — Powered by Financial Modeling Prep API

### Understanding the Signals
- **BREAKOUT** (🟢) — Vol ≥3× AND at ≥85% of 52W high
- **WATCH** (🟡) — Vol ≥2× or at ≥80% of 52W high with elevated volume
- **BUILDING** (🟠) — Vol ≥1.3× — early accumulation phase
